{% trans 'About Doing' %}

{% blocktrans %} In Cameroon 80% of the companies created disappear after 2 years (Ecam, 2015), cause of Poor supervision of young entrepreneurs, lack of financing, lack of assistance during the setting up and implementation of the project, etc...
DOING is the solution to youth entrepreneurship in Cameroon, it is a competition that brings together young people from the national territory with project ideas in various fields (catering, art and culture, machinery, agro-industry and ICT). It takes place in 04 main stages, candidatures, preselections, eliminatories and the final with as price the financing, the realization and the piloting of the winning projects. {% endblocktrans %}
